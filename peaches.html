About

Peaches is a two year old dutch rabbit. I have had her for about a year and a half now.

Dutch rabbits are among the smartest breed of rabbits, and are able to learn their name, learn tricks, and be fully litterbox trained.

Unfortunately, Peaches is not as smart as the average Dutch rabbit.

More Information

Despite popular belief, rabbits are not really the simplest of pets to take care of. They have complex diets, unique needs, and require lots of attention and exercise.
